Frequently Asked Questions

  • What are your office hours?

    Our office is open Monday through Friday from 9am to 5pm, and on Saturdays from 9am to 12pm.

  • Do you accept insurance?

    Yes, we accept most major insurance plans. Please bring your insurance card with you to your appointment (we also accept cash).

  • How often should I have my teeth cleaned?

    We recommend having your teeth cleaned twice a year, or more often if you have certain dental conditions.

  • Do you offer teeth whitening services?

    Yes, we offer teeth whitening treatments to help brighten your smile.

If you have any other questions, please contact us.
