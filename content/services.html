Our Services

  • Teeth Cleaning

    Our dental hygienists will clean your teeth thoroughly, removing plaque and tartar buildup to help prevent tooth decay and gum disease.

  • Fillings

    If you have a cavity, we can fill it with a tooth-colored composite material that will blend in with your natural teeth.

  • Extractions

    If a tooth is too damaged or decayed to be repaired, we may recommend extraction to prevent further damage or infection.

  • Root Canals

    If you have a tooth that is infected or abscessed, we may recommend a root canal to save the tooth and prevent further damage to surrounding teeth.
